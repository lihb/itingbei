17**超声波检查：主要看胎儿外观发育是否有较大问题，医师会仔细量胎儿的头围、腹围、大腿骨长度及检视脊柱是否有先天性异常。最令准妈妈期待的首次胎动，约在18-20周出现。**Utrasound Scanning:check the fetus growth condition，fetus head perimeter，abdominal perimeter,leg bone and spine development are checked. You will heard your baby's heart beat sound during the scanning.	**0
21**妊娠糖尿病筛检：如果查出患有妊娠糖尿病，要采取饮食及注射胰岛素来控制，不可使用口服降糖药，以免影响胎儿。**Gestational diabetes screening: If you are diagnosed with gestational diabetes, you should take a diet and inject insulin to control, do not use oral hypoglycemic agents, so as not to affect the fetus.**0
25**四维排畸检查：在这个时段胎儿的肢体及各主要脏器已经全部发育，羊水较适合做胎儿畸形筛查。**Four-dimensional dysmorphia examination: fetal limbs and the main organs have all developed in this period, and amniotic fluid is suitable for fetal malformations screening.**0
29**子痫前症检查：检查是否有水肿现象，如果测量结果发现准妈妈的血压偏高，又出现蛋白尿、全身水肿等情况时，准妈妈须多加留意，以免有子痫前症的危险。**Preeclampsia examination: check whether  there is any phenomenon of edema, if you are found to be with high blood pressure,and proteinuria, systemic edema, etc.,you need to pay more attention to avoid the risk of preeclampsia.**0
33**超声波检查：评估胎儿当时的体重及发育状况，并预估胎儿至足月生产时的重量。一旦发现胎儿体重不足，准妈妈就应多补充一些营养素；若发现胎儿过重，准妈妈在饮食上就要稍加控制，以免日后需要剖宫生产，或在生产过程中出现胎儿难产情形。**Utrasound Scanning:Evaluate the fetus weight and growth condition, and forcast the fetus weight. If your fetus weight is lower than the standard index, you should enhance the nutrition, if your fetus weight is higher than the standard index, you should control the nutrition.**0
36**为生产做准备：此时产检以每周一次为原则，并持续监视胎儿状态。**Prepare for labor: go to hospital every week and monitor the fetus condition.**0
